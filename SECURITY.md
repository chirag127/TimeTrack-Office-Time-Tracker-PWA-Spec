# Security Policy
## Introduction
The TimeGuard-Office-Time-Tracker-PWA prioritizes the security and integrity of user data. This policy outlines our guidelines and procedures for ensuring the confidentiality, availability, and integrity of our users' information.
## Reporting Security Vulnerabilities
If you discover a security vulnerability in TimeGuard-Office-Time-Tracker-PWA, please report it to us at [https://github.com/chirag127/TimeGuard-Office-Time-Tracker-PWA/issues](https://github.com/chirag127/TimeGuard-Office-Time-Tracker-PWA/issues). We appreciate your assistance in helping us maintain the security of our application.
## Security Features
* Encryption: We use industry-standard encryption to protect user data both in transit and at rest.
* Access Control: Role-based access control ensures that users can only access features and data they are authorized to.
* Regular Updates: We perform regular security audits and updates to ensure our dependencies and code are secure and up-to-date.
## Best Practices
* Keep your browser and operating system up-to-date with the latest security patches.
* Use a strong, unique password for your account and consider enabling two-factor authentication.
* Be cautious of phishing attempts and never provide your login credentials to unauthorized parties.
## Incident Response
In the event of a security incident, our response team will promptly assess the situation, contain the damage, and notify affected users if necessary. We will also take measures to prevent similar incidents from occurring in the future.
## Compliance
TimeGuard-Office-Time-Tracker-PWA complies with relevant regulations and standards, including GDPR and CCPA, to ensure the protection of user data.
## Contact
For any security-related questions or concerns, please contact us at [https://github.com/chirag127/TimeGuard-Office-Time-Tracker-PWA/issues](https://github.com/chirag127/TimeGuard-Office-Time-Tracker-PWA/issues).
